Familienerinnerungen

December 12, 2017

A short film following a small child, lost in an unrelenting jungle. A melancholy tale, focusing on loss, recovery, and survival in a cruel world. This was a practice in worldbuilding, character design, and silent storytelling.

Created using Adobe Illustrator, After Effects, and audacity. Certain art assets and audio are borrowed from other artists and outside sources.
