Cheese Shopping

April 19, 2018

A nonsensical fable in which a mouse in a high place goes on his grocery shopping routine in the only was a self-respecting mouse can. Lasers are involved.

Created using Autodesk Maya and audacity. Certain art assests and audio are borrowed from other artists and outside sources.
