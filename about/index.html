Andrew Doyon is a graphic designer, 3D modeler, programmer, web designer, voice actor and sound designer. He is currently a student at George Mason University, attending undergrad school in the Computer Game Design Major. He has produced numerous video games, websites and animations, many of which are self-made. He is the founder and owner of the corporation Spinning Bard Studios.

For three years after his initial enrollment, Andrew Doyon has produced many a work of digital art. He has been behind up to seven animations, ten video games, and ten works of digital art. He has served as a graphic designer for the non-profit Accounting For Good, and is a frequent contributor to the pool of art and design. He is setting his sights on expanding his sound design skills into the realm of music.

He remains the owner of Spinning Bard Studios. Following his graduation, he plans on elevating it to a full-blown animation studio, with which he can produce his own animated shows and projects.
